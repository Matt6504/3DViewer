Bike theft is on the rise; we all know somebody who knows somebody who's had their bike stolen. Here's some stats;
Since 2009 and the introduction of the Bike to Work scheme, reported incidents of bike thefts have doubled and continue to rise.
It is estimated that the amount of bikes stolen in 2013 was 20,000.
75% of national bike theft incidents occur in Dublin.

These statistics represent a challenge to active and sustainable travel ; 42% of people stop or reduce their frequency of cycling in response to exposure (either first or second hand) to a bike theft incident.

As a team, we understood that offering a solution which enhances the security of bike storage, and reduces the probability of a theft on campus, would contribute to the support of active travel to and from campus.
